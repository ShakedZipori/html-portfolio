How's Moving Castle

Howl's moving castle is a movie produced by my favorite director:
Hayao Miyazaki - the owner of Studio Ghibli.
This movie is my favorite because it has it all : romance,
fantasy, many plot twists and a very happy ending.

Spirited Away

Another master piece from Studio Ghibli. I like this movie because
the art is amazing, the fantasy is all over the place and the pplot
take some realy interesting twists. Also, has a very good ending.

Avatar

Avatar is a fantasy movie I started watching 10 years ago, when the first
movie in the series came out. This movie has magnificent technology involved
in order to deliver some high end effect and create a "realistic"
fantasy world. This movie touched me because it shown mankind's bad
